Research Projects

Automating Question Capture

Building high quality question banks will encourage reuse of questions and data, and transparency of research. This ESRC funded project seeks to utlise machine learning technologies to capture the content from existing questionnaires, reducing manual extry and increasing quality.

Predicting concepts from question text

his UCL funded project will take the outputs from the automation of question capture project and auto-tag with concepts which align with the European Language Social Science Thesaurus (ELSST).
